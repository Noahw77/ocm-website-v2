Founder & CEO: Steven Weinstein

Steven Weinstein

Investor, operator, and industry leader with 25+ years of experience.

Steven Weinstein has led research and investment initiatives across technology, media, and telecom. He has been recognized as an Institutional Investor All-Star and a Wall Street Journal “Best on the Street,” and has appeared on CNBC and Bloomberg Television. He has worked on notable IPOs including Alibaba, Mercado Libre, and Zillow, and previously served as a Partner and Senior Research Analyst at Pacific Crest Securities and as a Partner at M Science.

He founded Ochoco Capital Management to pursue a concentrated, long-term investing strategy aligned with investors through performance-based fees and significant personal capital commitment.

Outside of investing, Steven lives in Powell Butte, Oregon, where he enjoys hiking, paddle boarding, and skiing.
